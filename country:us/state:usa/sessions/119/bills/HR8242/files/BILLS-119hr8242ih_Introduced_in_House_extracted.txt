R. 8242IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 9, 2026Mr. Turner of Ohio (for himself, Ms. McDonald Rivet, Ms. Tenney, and Ms. Stevens) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and MeansA BILLTo amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the health coverage tax credit. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Health Coverage Tax Credit Reauthorization Act of 2026. 2.Extension of health coverage tax credit (a)In generalSection 35(b)(1)(B) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking before January 1, 2022 and inserting before January 1, 2030. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall apply to months beginning after December 31, 2021.
