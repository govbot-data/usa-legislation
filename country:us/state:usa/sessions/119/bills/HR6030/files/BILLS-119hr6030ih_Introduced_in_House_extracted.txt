R. 6030IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 12, 2025Mr. Hernández (for himself, Mr. Beyer, Mr. Torres of New York, Mr. Tonko, Mr. Goldman of New York, and Ms. Velázquez) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and CommerceA BILLTo include Puerto Rico in National Survey on Drug Use and Health, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Puerto Rico Substance Use and Health Data Collection Act. 
2.Inclusion of Puerto Rico in National Survey on Drug Use and HealthSection 505(d) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 290aa–4(d)) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (3)Inclusion of Puerto RicoBeginning with fiscal year 2026, annual surveys carried out under this section shall include Puerto Rico..
