Electing Members to certain standing committees of the House of Representatives.

Committee on the Judiciary:Mr. Kiley of California (to rank immediately after Mr. Fry).  Committee on Education and Workforce:Mr. Kiley of California (to rank immediately after Ms. Letlow).
