II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4336IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESApril 16 (legislative day, April 14), 2026Mr. Lee introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo amend the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 to repeal the antitrust exemption applicable to graduate medical resident matching programs.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Restoring Rights of Medical Residents Act.2.RepealerSection 207 of the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 (15 U.S.C. 37B) is repealed.3.Effective dateThis Act, and the amendment made by this Act, shall take effect on the March 18 that first occurs after the date of enactment of this Act.
