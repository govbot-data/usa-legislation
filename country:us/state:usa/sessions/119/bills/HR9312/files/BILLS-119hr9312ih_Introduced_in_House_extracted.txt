R. 9312IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 15, 2026Mr. Carter of Louisiana (for himself, Mr. Higgins of Louisiana, and Mr. Fields) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to include the processing of crawfish as agricultural labor or services, and for other purposes. 
1.Inclusion of crawfish processing as agricultural labor or servicesSection 101(a)(15)(H) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1101(a)(15)(H)) is amended by striking and the pressing of apples for cider on a farm and inserting the pressing of apples for cider on a farm, and the processing of crawfish (including washing, sorting, grading, whole-boiling, peeling, and transporting).
