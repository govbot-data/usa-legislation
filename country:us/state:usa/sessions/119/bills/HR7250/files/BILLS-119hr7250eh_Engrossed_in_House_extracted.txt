To reauthorize the Fort Peck Reservation Rural Water System Act of 2000. 
 
 
1.Reauthorization 
Section 9 of the Fort Peck Reservation Rural Water System Act of 2000 (Public Law 106–382; 114 Stat. 1457; 123 Stat. 2856; 128 Stat. 164; 132 Stat. 2906) is amended, in each of subsections (a)(1) and (b), by striking 2026 and inserting 2028.  Passed the House of Representatives June 2, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
