R. 6020IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 12, 2025Ms. Underwood (for herself, Ms. Kelly of Illinois, Mr. Krishnamoorthi, Ms. Schakowsky, Mr. Schneider, Mr. Foster, Mr. Davis of Illinois, and Mr. LaHood) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14855 South Van Dyke Road in Plainfield, Illinois, as the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building. 
1.Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14855 South Van Dyke Road in Plainfield, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building.
