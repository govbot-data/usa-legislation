I119th CONGRESS1st SessionH. R. 6037IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 12, 2025Mr. Langworthy introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and CommerceA BILLTo reauthorize the West Valley demonstration project. 
1.West valley demonstration projectSection 3(a) of the West Valley Demonstration Project Act (Public Law 96–368; 42 U.S.C. 2021a note) is amended by striking $75,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 through 2026 and inserting $150,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2037.
