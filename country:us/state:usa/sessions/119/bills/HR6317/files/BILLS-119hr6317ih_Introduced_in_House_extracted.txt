R. 6317IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 25, 2025Mr. Wittman (for himself, Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia, Mr. Scott of Virginia, Ms. McClellan, Mr. McGuire, Mr. Cline, Mr. Vindman, Mr. Beyer, Mr. Griffith, Mr. Subramanyam, and Mr. Walkinshaw) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, as the Patricia Paige Post Office.1.Patricia Paige Post Office(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Patricia Paige Post Office. (b) References Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Patricia Paige Post Office.
