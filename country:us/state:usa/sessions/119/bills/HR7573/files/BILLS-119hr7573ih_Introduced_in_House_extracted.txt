R. 7573IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 13, 2026Ms. Dexter (for herself and Mr. Carey) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and MeansA BILLTo amend title XVI of the Social Security Act to adjust the minimum monthly personal needs allowances for institutionalized individuals and couples under the supplemental security income program, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Modernize SSI Stipends Act.2.Adjustment of the minimum monthly personal needs allowances for institutionalized individuals and couples under the SSI program(a)Doubling of allowances(1)In generalSection 1611(e)(1)(B) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1382(e)(1)(B)) is amended—(A)in each of clauses (i) and (ii)(I) by striking $360 and inserting $720; and(B)in clause (iii), by striking $720 and inserting $1,440.(2)ApplicabilityThe amendments made by paragraph (1) shall apply with respect to benefits for months after December 31, 2025. (b) Application of cost-of-Living adjustments  (1) In general Section 1617(a)(1) of such Act (42 U.S.C. 1382f(a)(1)) is amended by striking and (b)(2) and inserting (b)(2), and (e)(1)(B).
        
        
          (2)
          Conforming amendments
 Section 1611(e)(1)(B) of such Act (42 U.S.C. 1382(e)(1)(B)) is amended in each of clauses (i) through (iii) by inserting (or, if greater, the amount determined under section 1617) after each dollar amount specified in such clauses.
        
        
          (3)
          Applicability
 The amendments made by this subsection shall apply with respect to benefit amount increases for months after December 31, 2025.
        
      
        (c)
        Pass-Through to State supplementary payments
        
          (1)
          In general
 Section 1618(g) of such Act (42 U.S.C. 1382g(g)) is amended—  (A) by striking 1987 each place it appears and inserting 2025; and
          
          
            (B)
 in paragraph (4)(B), by striking increased— and all that follows and inserting increased by the amount of all cost-of-living adjustments under section 1617 to benefits determined under section 1611(e)(1)(B) which have occurred after September 2025 and before that particular month..
          
        
        
          (2)
          Effective date
 The amendments made by paragraph (1) shall take effect on January 1, 2026.
