R. 6603IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 10, 2025Mr. Torres of New York (for himself and Ms. Clarke of New York) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural ResourcesA BILLTo amend the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act to waive entrance fees to national parks on Federal holidays, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Our Parks Act.2.Waiver of entrance fees on Federal holidaysSection 803(e) of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (16 U.S.C. 6802(e)) is amended by adding at the end the following:(3)Federal holidaysFor each unit of the National Park System or National Wildlife Refuge System at which an entrance fee is charged under paragraph (1), the Secretary shall not charge an entrance fee for entrance on a Federal holiday listed in section 6103 of title 5, United States Code..
