R. 6849IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 18, 2025Mr. DeSaulnier introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2121 Meridian Park Boulevard in Concord, California, as the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building. 
1.Carl Jefferson Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2121 Meridian Park Boulevard in Concord, California, shall be known and designated as the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building.
