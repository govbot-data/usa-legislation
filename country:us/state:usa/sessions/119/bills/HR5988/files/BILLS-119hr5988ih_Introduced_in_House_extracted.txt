R. 5988IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 7, 2025Mrs. Watson Coleman (for herself, Ms. Norton, Mr. Fields, Mr. Moulton, Mr. Jackson of Illinois, Ms. Craig, Mrs. McIver, Mr. Carson, Ms. Tlaib, Ms. Clarke of New York, Mr. Green of Texas, and Ms. Lee of Pennsylvania) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo amend section 2680 of title 18, United States Code, to provide for an exception to the Federal Tort Claims Act. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Ceasing Outrageous Restitution and Reimbursement Under President Trump Act or the CORRUPT Act. 
2.Exception to Federal tort claims actSection 2680 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:  (o)Any claim brought by the President of the United States or a covered relative of such President during the term for which such President shall have been elected. For the purposes of this subsection, a covered relative means a spouse, child, sibling, or in-law of the President..
