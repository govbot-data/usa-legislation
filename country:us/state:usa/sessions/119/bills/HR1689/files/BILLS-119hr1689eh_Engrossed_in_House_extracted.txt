119th CONGRESS2d Session 
H. R. 1689 
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 
AN ACT 
To require the Secretary of Homeland Security to designate Haiti for temporary protected status. 
 
 
1.Designation of Haiti for temporary protected statusNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of Homeland Security shall designate Haiti for temporary protected status until the date that is 3 months after January 20, 2029. Passed the House of Representatives April 16, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
