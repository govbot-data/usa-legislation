IV 
119th CONGRESS 
2d Session 
H. J. RES. 72 
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 
JOINT RESOLUTION 
Relating to a national emergency by the President on February 1, 2025.  
 
 
That pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (50 U.S.C. 1622), the national emergency declared by the finding of the President on February 1, 2025, in Executive Order 14193 (25 Fed. Reg. 02406), is hereby terminated.  Passed the House of Representatives February 11, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
