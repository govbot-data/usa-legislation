        A BILL
        To amend the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 to strengthen country of
            origin and method of production labeling for fish.
    
    
        
            1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Let Americans Buy with Explicit Labeling Act or the LABEL Act.
        
        
            2.
            Country of origin and method of production
                labeling for fish
 Section 282(c) of the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 (7 U.S.C. 1638a(c)) is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (3)
                    Labeling for fish
 In the case of a covered commodity that is farm-raised fish or wild fish, the information required by subsection (a) shall be provided to consumers by a means described in paragraph (1) in a conspicuous location, so as to render the information likely to be read and understood by a consumer under normal conditions of purchase, in a font size that is not smaller than the font size describing the farm-raised fish or wild fish on the package, display, holding unit, or bin.
                
                .
            
        
        
            3.
            Effective date
 This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
