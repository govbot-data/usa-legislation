R. 9315IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 15, 2026Mr. Grothman (for himself and Mr. Onder) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means, and in addition to the Committees on Small Business, and Transportation and Infrastructure, for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concernedA BILLTo amend the Small Business Act, the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, title 40, United States Code, and title IV of the Social Security Act to replace the term sheltered workshop with community rehabilitation program, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Workforce Dignity Act. 
2.Replacing the term sheltered workshop with community rehabilitation program 
(a)Programs for blind and handicapped individualsSection 15(c)(6) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 644(c)(6)) is amended by striking sheltered workshop each place it appears and inserting community rehabilitation program. (b)Task force on purchases from the blind and severely handicappedSection 133(c) of the Small Business Administration Reauthorization and Amendment Act of 1988 (15 U.S.C. 644 note) is amended by striking sheltered workshops and inserting community rehabilitation programs. 
(c)Deductions for personal exemptionsSection 152(d)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended— (1)by striking sheltered workshop each place it appears and inserting community rehabilitation program; 
(2)in subparagraph (A), by striking such workshop each place it appears and inserting such program; and (3)in subparagraph (B), by striking Sheltered workshop defined in the heading and inserting Community rehabilitation program defined. 
(d)Protection for veterans preference employeesSection 593 of title 40, United States Code, is amended— (1)by striking sheltered workshop each place it appears and inserting community rehabilitation program; 
(2)by striking sheltered workshops each place it appears and inserting community rehabilitation programs; and (3)in subsection (a)(2), by striking Sheltered workshop in the heading and inserting Community rehabilitation program.   
(e)Foster care programsSection 477(b)(3)(F) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 677(b)(3)(F)) is amended by striking sheltered workshops and inserting community rehabilitation programs.  (f)Rule of constructionThis Act shall be construed to amend Federal law to remove the term sheltered workshop without any intent to change the application of such law.
