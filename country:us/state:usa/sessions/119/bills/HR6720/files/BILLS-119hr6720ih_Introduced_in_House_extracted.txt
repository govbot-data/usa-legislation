R. 6720IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 15, 2025Ms. McDonald Rivet (for herself, Mr. Huizenga, and Mr. Riley of New York) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AgricultureA BILLTo amend the Farm Credit Act of 1971 to modify rural housing financing under that Act. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Fostering the Availability in Rural Markets of Home Loans Act of 2025 or the FARM Home Loans Act of 2025. 2.Rural housing financingSection 1.11(b) of the Farm Credit Act of 1971 (12 U.S.C. 2019(b)) is amended—
(1)in paragraph (2), by striking appurtenances and inserting appurtenances, including accessory dwelling units,; and (2)in paragraph (3), by striking 2,500 and inserting 10,000.
