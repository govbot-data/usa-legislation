R. 9447IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 24, 2026Ms. Van Duyne (for herself, Mr. Jackson of Texas, Mr. Steube, and Mr. Gill of Texas) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to provide for denaturalization of certain persons who provide support for terrorism. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Getting Terrorist Fanatics Out Act of 2026. 2.Denaturalization for terrorist offensesSection 340(e) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1451(e)) is amended— 
(1)by striking When a person shall be convicted and inserting the following:  (1)In generalWhen a person shall be convicted; and 
(2)by adding at the end the following:   (2)Terrorist offensesWhen a person shall be convicted under section 2339A or under section 2339B of title 18, United States Code, the court in which such conviction is had shall thereupon revoke, set aside, and declare void the final order admitting such person to citizenship, and shall declare the certificate of naturalization of such person to be canceled. Jurisdiction is hereby conferred on the courts having jurisdiction of the trial of such offence to make such adjudication..
