R. 8853IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 15, 2026Ms. McClellan (for herself and Mr. Deluzio) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and InfrastructureA BILLTo amend title 49, United States Code, to allow Amtrak to use grant funds to satisfy non-Federal share requirements of certain grant programs, and for other purposes. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Amtrak Grant Flexibility Act. 2.Eligibility of grant funds to satisfy non-Federal share requirements (a)National infrastructure project assistance grantsSection 6701(i)(2) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

(D)Allowable use of Amtrak grant funds

                            (i)
                            In general
 If Amtrak is the recipient of a grant under this section, Amtrak may use any Federal grant funds provided to Amtrak (other than grant funds awarded under the program and subject to any terms or conditions placed on such Federal grant funds) to carry out any activity described in subsection (h)(1), so long as the involvement of Amtrak in such activity is not limited to monetary contributions.
                        
(ii)Non-applicability of limitationAny Federal grant funds used by Amtrak pursuant to clause (i) may not be treated as Federal assistance for purposes of subparagraph (B).. (b)Consolidated rail infrastructure and safety improvements grantsSection 22907(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:

(5)Treatment of Amtrak grant fundsIf Amtrak is an applicant under this section, any Federal funds provided to Amtrak may be used to satisfy the non-Federal share requirements.. (c)Federal-State partnership for intercity passenger rail grantsSection 24911(f)(3) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking may use and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting the following:
may satisfy the non-Federal share requirements using—  (A) tickets and other revenues generated from its operations or other sources;
                    
(B)with respect to a project under this section associated the National Network, grant funds issued by the Secretary to Amtrak for use in activities associated with the National Network; and (C)with respect to a project under this section associated the Northeast Corridor, grant funds issued by the Secretary to Amtrak for use in activities associated with the Northeast Corridor..
