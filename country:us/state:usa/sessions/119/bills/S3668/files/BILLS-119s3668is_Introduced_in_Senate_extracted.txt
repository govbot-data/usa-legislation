II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 3668IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJanuary 15, 2026Mr. Murphy introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Scotland, Connecticut.1.Single, unique ZIP code for Scotland, ConnecticutNot later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code, to be numbered 06264, applicable to the area encompassing solely Scotland, Connecticut.
