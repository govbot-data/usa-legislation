A BILL
To amend title 18, United States Code, to authorize capital punishment for certain sexual abuse offenses against children.


1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Death Penalty for Child Rapists Act. 2.Authorization of death penalty for certain sexual abuse offenses against children (a)Aggravated sexual abuseSection 2241(c) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking shall be fined under this title and imprisoned for not less than 30 years or for life and inserting shall be punished by death or imprisonment for any term of years or for life and by a fine under this title.

                (b)
                Sexual abuse of a minor
 Section 2243(a) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking shall be fined under this title, imprisoned not more than 15 years, or both and inserting shall be punished by death or imprisonment for any term of years or for life, by a fine under this title, or by both.
            

                (c)
                Abusive sexual contact
 Section 2244 of title 18, United States Code, is amended—  (1) in paragraph (a)(3), by striking shall be fined under this title, imprisoned not more than two years, or both and inserting shall be punished by death or imprisonment for any term of years or for life, by a fine under this title, or by both;
                
                
                    (2)
 in paragraph (a)(5), by striking shall be fined under this title and imprisoned for any term of years or for life and inserting shall be punished by death or imprisonment for any term of years or for life, by a fine under this title, or by both; and
                
                
                    (3)
 in subsection (c), by—  (A) striking (other than subsection (a)(5)); and
                    
                    
                        (B)
 striking maximum term of imprisonment that may be imposed for the offense shall be twice that otherwise provided in this section and inserting maximum punishment that may be imposed for the offense shall be death or imprisonment for any term of years or for life.

3.Authorization of death penalty for offense of rape of a child under the uniform code of military justiceSection 920b(a) of title 10, United States Code (article 120b(a) of the Uniform Code of Military Justice), is amended by inserting by death, or such other punishment after shall be punished. 4.SeverabilityIf any provision of this Act or any amendment made by this Act, or the application of a provision of this Act or an amendment made by this Act to any person or circumstance, is held to be unconstitutional, the remainder of this Act, and the application of the provision or amendment to any person or circumstance, shall not be affected by the holding.
