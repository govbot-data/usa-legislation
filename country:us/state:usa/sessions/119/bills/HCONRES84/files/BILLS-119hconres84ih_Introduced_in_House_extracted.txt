RES. 84IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 13, 2026Ms. Tlaib submitted the following concurrent resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign AffairsCONCURRENT RESOLUTIONDirecting the President pursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution to remove United States Armed Forces from Lebanon. 
 
1.Removal of United States Armed Forces from LebanonPursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution (50 U.S.C. 1544(c)), Congress directs the President to remove the United States Armed Forces from Lebanon by not later than the date that is 7 days after the date of the adoption of this concurrent resolution.
