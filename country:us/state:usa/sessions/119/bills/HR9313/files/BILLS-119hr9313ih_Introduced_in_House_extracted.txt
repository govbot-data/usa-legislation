R. 9313IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 15, 2026Mrs. Dingell (for herself and Ms. Tlaib) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AppropriationsA BILLTo appropriate $500,000,000 to the low-income household water assistance program under section 2912 of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Water Access Act.
2.Appropriation for LIHWAPThere is appropriated to the Secretary of Health and Human Services for fiscal year 2027, $500,000,000 for the low-income household water assistance program under section 2912 of subtitle K of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 (15 U.S.C. 9058b).
