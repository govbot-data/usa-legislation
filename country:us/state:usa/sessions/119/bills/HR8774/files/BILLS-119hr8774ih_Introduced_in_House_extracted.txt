R. 8774IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 12, 2026Mr. Williams of Texas (for himself, Mr. Hunt, Mr. Fallon, Mr. Haridopolos, and Mr. McGuire) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Homeland SecurityA BILLTo amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize the use of certain financial assistance for vehicle security enhancement upgrades, and for other purposes. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Bulletproof Law Enforcement Vehicles Act. 2.DHS vehicle security enhancement upgradesSection 432 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (6 U.S.C. 240) is amended—
(1)by redesignating subsection (e) as subsection (f); and (2)by inserting after subsection (d) the following new subsection:

(e)Certain usesThe Secretary shall authorize the use of financial assistance under subsection (d)(2) for vehicle security enhancement upgrades, including for bulletproof windows..
