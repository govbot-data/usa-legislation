AN ACT
To amend title 5, United States Code, to authorize the increase of the retirement age in the United States Capitol Police.

 
1.Increase in retirement age of United States Capitol Police officers 
(a)Civil Service Retirement SystemSection 8335(c) of title 5, United States Code, is amended by striking becomes 60 years of age and inserting attains an age determined by the Board that shall be not less than 57 years of age and not more than 65 years of age. (b)Federal Employees’ Retirement SystemSection 8425(c) of title 5, United States Code, is amended by striking becomes 60 years of age and inserting attains an age determined by the Board that shall be not less than 57 years of age and not more than 65 years of age.   
Passed the House of Representatives April 27, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
