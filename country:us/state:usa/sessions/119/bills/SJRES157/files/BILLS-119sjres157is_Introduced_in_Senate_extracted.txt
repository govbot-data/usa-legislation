RES. 157IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 26, 2026Mr. Welch introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5,
      United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
      relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Bulletin 2012-04: Lending
        discrimination (April 18, 2012).
    
 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Bulletin 2012-04: Lending discrimination (April 18, 2012) (90 Fed. Reg. 20084 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
