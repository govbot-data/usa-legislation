R. 7365IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 4, 2026Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1225 Kempsville Road in Virginia Beach, Virginia, as the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building.1.Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1225 Kempsville Road in Virginia Beach, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building.
