R. 9464IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 25, 2026Ms. Goodlander (for herself and Mr. Pappas) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AgricultureA BILLTo prohibit the Secretary of Agriculture from closing any research and development facility of the Forest Service. 
1.Prohibition on closure of Forest Service research and development facilitiesThe Secretary of Agriculture may not close any research and development facility of the Forest Service, including through implementation of a reorganization plan, except as expressly authorized by an Act of Congress enacted after the date of enactment of this Act.
