R. 9549IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 30, 2026Ms. Jayapal (for herself, Mr. Davidson, Mr. Garamendi, and Mr. Moulton) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Armed ServicesA BILLTo amend title 10, United States Code, the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022, and the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 to repeal certain reporting requirements related to unfunded priorities, and for other purposes. 
1.Repealing reporting requirements related to unfunded priorities 
(a)In generalTitle 10, United States Code, is amended by repealing each of the following sections: (1)Section 222a. 
(2)Section 222f. (3)Section 240i. 
(4)Section 4815. (5)Section 5513. 
(6)Section 6288. (b)Small business innovation research and technology transfer programSection 865 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 (Public law 117–81; 10 U.S.C. 4901 note) is repealed.  
(c)Military construction projectsSection 2806 of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (division B of Public Law 115–91; 10 U.S.C. 222a note) is repealed.
