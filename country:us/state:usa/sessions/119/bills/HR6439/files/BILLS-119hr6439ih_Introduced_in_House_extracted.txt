R. 6439IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 4, 2025Mr. Tony Gonzales of Texas (for himself, Mr. Guest, Mr. Kelly of Pennsylvania, Mr. Alford, Mr. Stauber, and Mr. McGuire) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo expand eligibility for special overtime pay to U.S. Border Patrol agents classified above grade GS–12.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Border Patrol Supervisors Retention Act.2.Establishment of higher rates of regularly scheduled overtime pay for certain U.S. Border Patrol agentsSection 5550(h) of title 5, United States Code, is amended—(1)in the subsection heading, by striking GS–12 Border Patrol agents and inserting Border Patrol agents classified from grade GS–12 through GS–15; and(2)in paragraph (1), by striking a position at grade GS–12 and inserting a position from grade GS–12 through GS–15.
