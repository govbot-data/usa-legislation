R. 9265IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 11, 2026Mr. Carson (for himself, Ms. Norton, Mr. Cohen, Ms. Brown, and Ms. Titus) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and WorkforceA BILLTo amend the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act to require participation in the summer EBT program as a condition of participating in the school lunch program, and for other purposes. 
1.Summer EBT participation 
(a)Program participationSection 13A(a) of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1762(a)) is amended— (1)in the matter preceding paragraph (1)— 
(A)by striking electing to participate in such program and inserting described in paragraph (2); (B)by striking beginning with summer 2024 and annually; 
(C)by striking thereafter; and (D)by striking The Secretary shall and inserting the following: 
 
(1)In generalThe Secretary shall; (2)by redesignating paragraphs (1) and (2) as subparagraphs (A) and (B); and 
(3)by adding at the end the following:  (2)States and covered Indian Tribal organizations (A)Summers 2024–26With respect to summers 2024, 2025, and 2026, States and covered Indian Tribal organizations described in this paragraph are States and covered Indian Tribal organizations electing to participate in the program established under paragraph (1). 
(B)Summer 2027 and beyondWith respect to summer 2027 and each summer thereafter, States and covered Indian Tribal organizations described in this paragraph are— (i)States participating in the school lunch program under this Act; and 
(ii)covered Indian Tribal organizations— (I)participating in the school lunch program under this Act; or 
(II)electing to participate in the program established under paragraph (1).. (b)Program requirementsSection 13A(c)(1) of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1762(c)(1)) is amended, in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking elect to. 
(c)Administrative and management plan 
(1)In generalSection 13A(g) of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1762(g)) is amended— (A)by striking Beginning in 2024 and inserting the following: 
 
(1)Summers 2024–26With respect to the years 2024, 2025, and 2026; and (B)by adding at the end the following: 
 
(2)Summer 2027 and beyondBeginning in 2027, each State required to participate in the program under this section shall comply with the requirements under section 13(n)(3).. (2)Conforming amendmentsSection 13(n) of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1761(n)(2)) is amended— 
(A)in paragraph (2)— (i)in the heading for such paragraph, by striking and beyond and inserting through summer 2026; and 
(ii)in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking Beginning in 2024 and inserting With respect to the years 2024, 2025, and 2026; and (B)by adding at the end the following: 
 
(3)Summer 2027 and beyondBeginning in 2027, each State desiring to participate in the program under this section or required to participate in the summer EBT program under section 13A shall— (A)with respect to the program under this section, notify the Secretary by January 1 of each year of its intent to administer such program; and 
(B)with respect to the program under this section and the summer EBT program under section 13A, submit for approval by February 15 a management and administration plan for the applicable program for the fiscal year, which shall include, as applicable, the information described in subparagraphs (A) through (F) of paragraph (2).. (d)Participation requirementSection 13A of the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1762(g)) is amended—
