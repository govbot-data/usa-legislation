II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 3799IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESFebruary 5, 2026Mr. Marshall (for himself, Mrs. Gillibrand, and Ms. Baldwin) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and PensionsA BILLTo amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize the Healthy Start Initiative.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Healthy Start Reauthorization Act of 2026.2.Reauthorization of Healthy Start InitiativeSection 330H(e)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 254c–8(e)(1)) is amended by striking appropriated and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting appropriated $145,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030..
