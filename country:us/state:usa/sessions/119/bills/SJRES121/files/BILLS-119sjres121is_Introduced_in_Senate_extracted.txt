RES. 121IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 9, 2026Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public WorksJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Oil and Natural Gas Sector Climate Review: Extension of Deadlines in Standards of Performance for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources and Emissions Guidelines for Existing Sources.That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Oil and Natural Gas Sector Climate Review: Extension of Deadlines in Standards of Performance for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources and Emissions Guidelines for Existing Sources (90 Fed.
