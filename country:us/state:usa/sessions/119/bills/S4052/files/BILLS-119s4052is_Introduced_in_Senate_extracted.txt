II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4052IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 11, 2026Mr. Schmitt (for himself and Mr. Hawley) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 203 North Clay Street in Marshfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office.1.Edwin P. Hubble Post Office(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 203 North Clay Street in Marshfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office.
