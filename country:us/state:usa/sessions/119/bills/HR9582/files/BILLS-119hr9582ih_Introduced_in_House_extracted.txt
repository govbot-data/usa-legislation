R. 9582IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJuly 2, 2026Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia (for herself and Mr. Davis of North Carolina) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural ResourcesA BILLTo authorize the location of the National Service Animals Monument on the National Mall, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the National Service Animals Monument Location Act. 
2.Authorization of location of National Service Animals Monument Notwithstanding section 8908(c) of title 40, United States Code, and section 704(b) of division DD of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 (40 U.S.C. 8903 note), the commemorative work authorized by such section 704 shall be located within the Reserve (as defined in section 8902(a) of title 40, United States Code).
