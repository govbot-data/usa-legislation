II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4623IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMay 21, 2026Mr. Warner (for himself and Mr. Kaine) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, as the Patricia Paige Post Office.1.Patricia Paige Post Office(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Patricia Paige Post Office.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Patricia Paige Post Office.
