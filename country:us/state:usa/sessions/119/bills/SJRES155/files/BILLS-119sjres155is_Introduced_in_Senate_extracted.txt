RES. 155IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 26, 2026Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws.That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws (90 Fed. Reg. 48710 (October 28, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
