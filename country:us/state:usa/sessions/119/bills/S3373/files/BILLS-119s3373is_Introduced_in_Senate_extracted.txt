II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3373IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 4, 2025Ms. Duckworth (for herself and Mr. Durbin) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14855 South Van Dyke Road in Plainfield, Illinois, as the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building.1.Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14855 South Van Dyke Road in Plainfield, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Staff Sergeant Jose Dueñez Jr. Post Office Building.
