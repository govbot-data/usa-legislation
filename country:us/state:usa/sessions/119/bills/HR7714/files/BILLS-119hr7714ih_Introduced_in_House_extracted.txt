R. 7714IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 25, 2026Mr. Stanton (for himself and Ms. Ansari) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 101 North Colorado Street in Chandler, Arizona, as the Mayor Coy Payne Memorial Post Office Building. 
1.Mayor Coy Payne Memorial Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 101 North Colorado Street in Chandler, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Mayor Coy Payne Memorial Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Mayor Coy Payne Memorial Post Office Building.
