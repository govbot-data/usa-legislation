AN ACT
To amend title 18, United States Code, to prohibit fraud in connection with posting bail.


1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Keeping Violent Offenders Off Our Streets Act of 2025. 2.Fraud in connection with posting bailSection 1033(f)(1)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting before the comma the following: (including the posting of monetary bail, criminal bail bonds, and Federal immigration bail bonds).
Passed the House of Representatives May 14, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
