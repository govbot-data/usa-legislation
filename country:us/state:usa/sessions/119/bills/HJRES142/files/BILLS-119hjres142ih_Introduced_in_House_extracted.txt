Income and Franchise Tax Conformity and Revision Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 (D.C. Act 26–217), enacted by the Council of the District of Columbia on December 20, 2025, and transmitted to Congress pursuant to section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act on December 30, 2025.
