IA119th CONGRESS2d SessionH. J. RES. 150IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 17, 2026Mr. Meeks submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign AffairsJOINT RESOLUTIONTerminating the national emergency declared to impose global tariffs. 
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (50 U.S.C. 1622), the national emergency declared on April 2, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14257 (90 Fed. Reg. 15041) is terminated effective on the date of the enactment of this joint resolution.
