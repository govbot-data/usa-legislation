        (a)
        Abolishing the Anti-Weaponization Fund
 The Anti-Weaponization Fund established by the Attorney General in connection with the disposition in Trump v. Internal Revenue Service, No. 1:26-cv-20609–KMW (S.D. Fla.), is abolished.
      
        (b)
        Nullification of order relating to release of certain claims
 The order of the Attorney General issued on May 19, 2026, relating to the release of certain claims shall have no force or effect.
