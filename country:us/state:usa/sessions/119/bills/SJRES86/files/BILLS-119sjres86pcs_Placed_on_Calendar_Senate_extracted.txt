JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; South Dakota; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period.


That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; South Dakota; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period (90 Fed. Reg. 41893 (August 28, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.   December 9, 2025 Committee discharged, by petition, pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 802(c), and placed on the calendar
