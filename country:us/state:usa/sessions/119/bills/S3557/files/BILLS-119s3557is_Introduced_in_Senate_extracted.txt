II119th CONGRESS
 1st SessionS. 3557IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 17, 2025Mr. Markey (for himself, Mr. Van Hollen, Mr. Welch, Mr. Wyden, Mr. Sanders, Mr. Schiff, Mr. Booker, Mr. Luján, Ms. Klobuchar, Mr. Padilla, and Mr. Durbin) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and TransportationA BILLTo prohibit the use of Federal funds to implement the Executive order entitled Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the States' Right to Regulate AI Act.2.No Federal funds for Executive order relating to State regulation of artificial intelligenceNo Federal funds may be used to implement, administer, or enforce the Executive order entitled Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence, issued on December 11, 2025.
