R. 7657IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 24, 2026Mrs. Foushee (for herself and Mr. Mackenzie) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Armed ServicesA BILLTo direct the Secretary of Defense to provide funds to the National Diaper Bank Network for the establishment of a Military Family Diaper Fund to provide diapers and diapering supplies to military families in need, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Military Family Diaper Partnership Act.
2.Provision of funds for establishment of Military Family Diaper Fund 
(a)Provision of FundsThe Secretary of Defense shall seek to enter an agreement with the National Diaper Bank Network to provide to the Network $1,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2030 for the purpose of establishing a military family diaper fund to distribute diapers and diapering supplies to military families. (b)Use of fundsAn agreement under subsection (a) shall provide that— 
(1)the National Diaper Bank Network shall establish a military family diaper fund and deposit funds received from the Secretary under the agreement into such fund; (2)the National Diaper Bank Network shall deposit into the military family diaper fund matching funds or in-kind contributions from non-Federal Government sources that are of a value equal or greater to the amount of funds provided by the Secretary under the agreement; 
(3)funds or in-kind contributions in the military family diaper fund shall only be provided to members of the National Diaper Bank Network who have engaged in the distribution of diapers or diapering supplies in an area located within 20 miles of a military installation for a period of not less than five years; (4)such funds or in-kind contributions shall only be used— 
(A)to provide diapers and diapering supplies to military families in need of diapers or diapering supplies; or (B)for technical assistance and evaluation provided by the National Diaper Bank Network; and 
(5)the National Diaper Bank Network shall submit to the Secretary of Defense an annual report on the activities carried out using the military family diaper fund during the preceding fiscal year.
