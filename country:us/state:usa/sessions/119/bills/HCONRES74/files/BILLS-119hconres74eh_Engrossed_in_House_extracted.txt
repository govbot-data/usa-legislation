IV 
119th CONGRESS 
2d Session 
H. CON. RES. 74 
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 
Providing for a joint session of Congress to receive a message from the President. 
 
 
That the two Houses of Congress assemble in the Hall of the House of Representatives on Tuesday, February 24, 2026, at 9 p.m., for the purpose of receiving such communication as the President of the United States shall be pleased to make to them.  Passed the House of Representatives February 10, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
