RESOLUTION 
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the engrossment of H.R. 7147. 
 
 
That the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall, in the engrossment of the bill H.R. 7147, make the following correction: In section 230(b), strike in this Act or any other act and insert for the Department of Homeland Security in this or any prior Act, and such correction shall be incorporated in the engrossment of H.R. 7148 pursuant to House Resolution 1014.  Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
