R. 8685IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 7, 2026Mr. Gooden (for himself and Mr. Steube) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo amend section 1751 of title 18, United States Code, to include the death penalty as punishment for an attempt to assassinate certain individuals. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Protect the Presidency Act.  
2.Punishment for assassination of certain individualsSection 1751(c) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting death or before imprisonment.
