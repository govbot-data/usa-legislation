R. 7229IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJanuary 22, 2026Mr. Moylan (for himself and Ms. King-Hinds) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Small BusinessA BILLTo amend the Small Business Act to provide for loan guarantees to certain small businesses, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Territorial SBA Loan Guaranty Adjustment Act of 2026.2.Loan guarantees for covered territory businesses(a)In generalSection 7(a)(2) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 636(a)(2)) is amended by adding at the end the following:(G)Participation in loan to covered territory business(i)In generalExcept as provided in clause (ii), in an agreement to participate in a loan to a covered territory business on a deferred basis under this subsection, the participation by the Administration shall be 90 percent.(ii)ExceptionsClause (i) shall not apply with respect to a loan under paragraph (9), (14)(A), (16), (31), or (34), or under a pilot program carried out pursuant to paragraph (25)..(b)Conforming amendmentSection 7(a)(2)(A) of such Act is amended by striking and (F) and inserting (F), and (G).
