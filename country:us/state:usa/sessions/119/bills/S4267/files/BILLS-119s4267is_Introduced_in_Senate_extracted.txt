II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4267IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 26, 2026Ms. Lummis (for herself and Mr. Cotton) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Energy and Natural ResourcesA BILLTo codify certain Executive orders relating to nuclear energy.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Strengthening American Nuclear Energy Act of 2026.2.Codification of Executive orders relating to nuclear energyThe following Executive orders, signed on May 23, 2025, shall have the force and effect of law:(1)Executive Order 14301 (90 Fed. Reg. 22591 (May 29, 2025); relating to reforming nuclear reactor testing at the Department of Energy).(2)Executive Order 14299 (90 Fed. Reg. 22581 (May 29, 2025); relating to deploying advanced nuclear reactor technologies for national security).(3)Executive Order 14300 (90 Fed. Reg. 22587 (May 29, 2025); relating to ordering the reform of the Nuclear Regulatory Commission).(4)Executive Order 14302 (90 Fed. Reg. 22595 (May 29, 2025); relating to reinvigorating the nuclear industrial base).
