An Act
To require the Federal Communications Commission to issue an order providing that a shark attack is an event for which a wireless emergency alert may be transmitted, and for other purposes.June 26, 2026[S. 1003]

  Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representa­tives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,Lulu’s Law.47 USC 1201 note.
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.  This Act may be cited as “Lulu’s Law”.
SEC. 2. WIRELESS EMERGENCY ALERTS.(a) Definition.—In this section, the term “Alert Message” has the meaning given the term in section 10.10(a) of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations, or any successor regulation.
(b) Deadline.Requirement.—Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Federal Communications Commission shall issue an order to provide that a shark attack is an event for which an Alert Message may be transmitted.


Approved June 26, 2026.
