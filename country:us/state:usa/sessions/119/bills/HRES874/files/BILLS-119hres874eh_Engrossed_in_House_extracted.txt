IV 
119th CONGRESS 
1st Session 
H. RES. 874 
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

November 12, 2025
 
RESOLUTION 
 
 
 
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Richard B. Cheney, a former Vice President of the United States of America. That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.  Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
