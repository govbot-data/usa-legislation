R. 7849IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMarch 5, 2026Mrs. Spartz introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and CommerceA BILLTo amend the Clean Air Act to exempt nonroad engines and vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes from the emission standards of that Act, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Farm Equipment Safety Act.2.Exemption of nonroad farm engines and vehicles from Clean Air Act emission standardsSection 213 of the Clean Air Act (42 U.S.C. 7547) is amended by adding at the end the following:(e)ExemptionThe standards under this section do not apply to emissions from nonroad engines and nonroad vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes..
