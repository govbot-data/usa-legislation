(a).(c)DefinitionsFor purposes of this section—(1)Asset-backed securities trust(A)In generalThe term asset-backed securities trust means any grantor trust—(i)for which the assets of the trust are trust identified by the Secretary (pursuant to regulations or other guidance) as appropriate to exclude from the application of subsection (b)(2).(2)Deemed ownerThe term deemed owner means, with respect to any trust, any person who is treated as the owner of such trust (or a portion thereof) under subpart E of part 1 of subchapter J.(d)Rule of constructionFor purposes of subsection (a), a
