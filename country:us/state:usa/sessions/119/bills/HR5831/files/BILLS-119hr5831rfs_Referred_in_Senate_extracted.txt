R. 5831IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESApril 15 (legislative day, April 14), 2026Received; read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsAN ACTTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 306 South Main Street in Waupaca, Wisconsin, as the Master Sergeant Melvin O.
