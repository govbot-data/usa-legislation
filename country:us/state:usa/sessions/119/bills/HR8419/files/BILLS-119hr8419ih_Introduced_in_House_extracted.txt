R. 8419IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 21, 2026Mr. Moskowitz (for himself, Mr. Frost, Mrs. Cherfilus-McCormick, Ms. Wilson of Florida, Ms. Wasserman Schultz, Ms. Castor of Florida, Mr. Soto, Ms. Lois Frankel of Florida, Ms. Salazar, Mr. Donalds, Mrs. Luna, Mr. Mills, and Mr. Diaz-Balart) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3255 Northwest 94th Avenue in Coral Springs, Florida, as the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building. 
1.Nancy Metayer Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3255 Northwest 94th Avenue in Coral Springs, Florida, shall be known and designated as the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building.
