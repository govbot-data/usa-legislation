II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 3714IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJanuary 28, 2026Ms. Hassan (for herself and Mr. Scott of South Carolina) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on FinanceA BILLTo amend title V of the Social Security Act to extend funding for the family-to-family health information centers.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Family-to-Family Reauthorization Act of 2026.2.Extension of funding for family-to-family health information centersSection 501(c)(1)(A) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 701(c)(1)(A)), as amended by section 6303 of division F of the Continuing Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative Branch, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act, 2026 (Public Law 119–37; 139 Stat. 636) is amended—(1)in clause (viii), by striking and at the end;(2)in clause (ix), by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and(3)by adding at the end the following:(x)for the period beginning on January 31, 2026, and ending on September 30, 2026, an amount equal to the pro rata portion of the amount appropriated for fiscal year 2025; and(xi)$6,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2030..
