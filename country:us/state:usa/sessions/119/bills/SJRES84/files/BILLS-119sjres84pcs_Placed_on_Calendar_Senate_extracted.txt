JOINT RESOLUTION 
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act; Marketplace Integrity and Affordability. 
 
 
 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act; Marketplace Integrity and Affordability (90 Fed. Reg. 27074 (June 25, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.    December 9, 2025 Committee discharged, by petition, pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 802(c), and placed on the calendar
