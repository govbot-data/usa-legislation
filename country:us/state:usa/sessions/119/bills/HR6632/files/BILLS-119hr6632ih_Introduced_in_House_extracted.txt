R. 6632IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 11, 2025Ms. Escobar introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AppropriationsA BILLTo make appropriations for the replacement of the air traffic control tower at El Paso International Airport in El Paso, Texas. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the El Paso Air Traffic Control Tower Modernization Act. 2.Appropriation for air traffic control towerThere are hereby appropriated, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as may be necessary for the replacement of the El Paso Air Traffic Control Tower at the El Paso International Airport in El Paso, Texas.
