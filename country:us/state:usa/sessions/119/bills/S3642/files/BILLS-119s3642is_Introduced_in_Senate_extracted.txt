II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 3642IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJanuary 14, 2026Mr. Marshall introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on FinanceA BILLTo direct the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services to investigate certain payment increases under State programs funded by the Department of Health and Human Services.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Welfare Abuse and Laundering Zillions Act or the WALZ Act.2.Requiring investigations of certain payment increases under State programs funded by the Department of Health and Human ServicesIn the case that the total amount paid to providers of services and suppliers under a State program that receives Federal financial assistance administered by the Secretary of Health and Human Services during any 6-month period increases by 10 percent or more as compared to that amount during the prior 6-month period, the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services shall open an investigation into such program.
