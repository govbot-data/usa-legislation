R. 6209IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 20, 2025Ms. Mace (for herself, Mr. Massie, Ms. Lofgren, and Mr. Baird) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AgricultureA BILLTo repeal section 781 of the Continuing Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative Branch, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act, 2026, relating to amendments to the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946, with respect to hemp.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the American Hemp Protection Act of 2025.2.Repeal of amendments to Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946, with respect to hempEffective November 12, 2025, section 781 of the Continuing Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative Branch, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act, 2026 (Public Law 119–37) is repealed.
