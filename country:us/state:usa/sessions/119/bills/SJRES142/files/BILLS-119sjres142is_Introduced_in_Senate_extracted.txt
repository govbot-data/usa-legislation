RES. 142IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 24, 2026Ms. Warren (for herself, Mr. Booker, and Mr. Blumenthal) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the National Credit Union Administration relating to Withdrawal of Fee Reporting Requirements.That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the National Credit Union Administration relating to Withdrawal of Fee Reporting Requirements (issued March 3, 2025, and a letter of opinion from the Government Accountability Office dated January 16, 2026, printed in the Congressional Record on February 9, 2026, on pages S530–532, concluding that withdrawal is a rule under the Congressional Review Act), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
