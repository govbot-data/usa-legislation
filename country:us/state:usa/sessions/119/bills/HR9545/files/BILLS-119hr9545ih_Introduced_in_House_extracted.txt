3.Federal adultery offense for officers of the United States and Members of Congress 
(a)OffenseChapter 93 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:  1925.Adultery by officers of the United States and Members of CongressWhoever, being an officer of the United States or a Member of Congress, in or affecting interstate or foreign commerce, knowingly engages in sexual intercourse (including genital-genital, oral-genital, anal-genital, or oral-anal, whether between persons of the same or opposite sex) with a person, and at the time of such conduct, the officer or Member or the person with whom the officer or Member engaged in such conduct, was married to another person, shall be fined under this title, and imprisoned for not more than one year, or both. . 
(b)Clerical amendmentThe table of sections for chapter 93 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:   1925. Adultery by officers of the United States and Members of Congress..
