H. R. 2550
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
AN ACT
To nullify the Executive Order relating to Exclusions from Federal Labor-Management Relations Programs, and for other purposes.


1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protect America's Workforce Act.  2.Nullification of Executive Order relating to Exclusions from Federal Labor-Management Relations ProgramsThe Executive order titled Exclusions from Federal Labor-Management Relations Programs, issued on March 27, 2025, shall have no force or effect and no Federal funds may be obligated or expended to carry out such Executive order.
3.Collective bargaining agreementsAny collective bargaining agreement in effect as of March 26, 2025, between any executive branch Federal agency and any labor organization that is an exclusive representative of Federal employees shall have full force and effect through the stated term of the applicable agreement. Passed the House of Representatives December 11, 2025.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
