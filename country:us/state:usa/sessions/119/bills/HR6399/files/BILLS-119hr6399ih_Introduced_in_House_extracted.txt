R. 6399IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 3, 2025Mr. Kennedy of Utah introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah.1.Single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, UtahNot later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Highland City, Utah.
