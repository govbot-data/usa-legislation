I119th CONGRESS1st SessionH. R. 5974IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 7, 2025Ms. Pettersen (for herself and Mr. Neguse) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on AppropriationsA BILLTo provide funding for personnel of Bureau of Prisons facilities. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Bureau of Prisons Pay Protection Act. 
2.Funding for personnel of Bureau of Prison facilitiesOut of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, there is appropriated such sums as are necessary to pay, during any period of a lapse in discretionary appropriations during any fiscal year, the salaries of the personnel, both correctional and non-correctional, of a facility of the Bureau of Prisons during such lapse.
