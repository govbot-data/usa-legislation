        JOINT RESOLUTION
        Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of
            title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Safety and
            Environmental Enforcement relating to Restoration of Names That Honor American
                Greatness; Gulf of America.
    
    
        
 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement relating to Restoration of Names That Honor American Greatness; Gulf of America (90 Fed. Reg. 44322 (September 15, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
