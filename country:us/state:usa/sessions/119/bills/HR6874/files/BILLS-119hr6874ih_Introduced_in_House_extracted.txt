and (2) consult with the Secretary of Energy, the Secretary of Commerce, and the heads of any other agency determined appropriate by the Secretary of Transportation to promote the effective use of predictive analytics, telematics, and other validated methodology tools in, and ensure interoperability of such tools across, Federal programs.
