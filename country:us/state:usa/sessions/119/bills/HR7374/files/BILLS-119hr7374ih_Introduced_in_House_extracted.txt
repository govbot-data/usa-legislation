1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Housing Without Fear Act of 2026. 2.Nullification of memorandum of understanding between HUD and DHSThe Secretary of Housing and Urban Development and the Secretary of Homeland Security may not carry out the memorandum of understanding signed by the Secretaries on March 24, 2025.
