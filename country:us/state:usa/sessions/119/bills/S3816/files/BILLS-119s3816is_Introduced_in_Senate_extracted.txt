II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 3816IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESFebruary 10, 2026Mr. Scott of South Carolina (for himself and Ms. Hassan) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on FinanceA BILLTo amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to permit a private cause of action for damages in the case of a group health plan which fails to provide for primary payment or appropriate reimbursement.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Repair Abuses of MSP Payments (RAMP) Act.2.Private cause of action for damages in the case of a group health plan which fails to provide for primary payment or appropriate reimbursementSection 1862(b)(3)(A) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395y(b)(3)(A)) is amended by striking primary plan and inserting group health plan (as defined in paragraph (1)(A)(v)).
