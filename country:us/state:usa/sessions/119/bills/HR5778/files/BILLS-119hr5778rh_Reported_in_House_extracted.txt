R. 5778[Report No. 119–412]IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESOctober 17, 2025Mrs. McIver (for herself, Mr. Bresnahan, and Ms. Velázquez) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Small BusinessDecember 17, 2025Additional sponsors: Mr. Fitzpatrick, Mr. Cisneros, and Ms. GoodlanderDecember 17, 2025Committed to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of the Union and ordered to be printedA BILLTo direct the Administrator of the Small Business Administration to improve outreach and education on employee ownership, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Improving SBA Engagement on Employee Ownership Act.2.Cooperative working group participationSection 7(a)(35) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 636(35)) is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:(C)Working groups and other engagementsThe Administrator (or a designee thereof) shall attend any working groups, meetings, fora, and any other engagements on cooperatives or employee ownership of business concerns that are hosted by any other Federal agency or office—(i)to which the Administrator (or designee thereof) is invited; or(ii)with which the Administrator (or a designee thereof) has had a previous engagement or relationship..3.Amendment to small business investment company program outreachSection 862(c) of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 (Public Law 115–232; 15 U.S.C. 648 note) is amended by inserting , and to the investors and limited partners of such companies, before to increase.4.Outreach and assistanceNot later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall carry out the requirements of section 862(e) of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 (Public Law 115–232; 15 U.S.C. 648 note) through the Small Business Employee Ownership and Cooperatives Promotion Program of the Administration.December 17, 2025Committed to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of the Union and ordered to be printed
