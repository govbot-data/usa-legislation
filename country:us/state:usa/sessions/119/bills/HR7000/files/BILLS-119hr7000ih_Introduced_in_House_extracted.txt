R. 7000IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJanuary 9, 2026Mr. Moore of Alabama (for himself, Ms. Fedorchak, Mr. Rulli, Mr. Hurd of Colorado, and Mr. Rose) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and CommerceA BILLTo amend the Clean Air Act to exclude a portable fuel container from the definition of a consumer or commercial product, and for other purposes. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Freedom to Fuel Act. 2.Portable fuel container exclusionSection 183(e)(1)(B) of the Clean Air Act (42 U.S.C. 7511b(e)(1)(B)) is amended— 
(1)in the first sentence, by striking The term and inserting the following:  (i)In generalThe term; and 
(2)by striking the second sentence and inserting the following:  (ii)ExclusionsThe term consumer or commercial product does not include the following: 
(I)A portable fuel container. (II)A fuel or fuel additive regulated under section 211. 
(III)A motor vehicle, non-road vehicle, or non-road engine as defined under section 216..
