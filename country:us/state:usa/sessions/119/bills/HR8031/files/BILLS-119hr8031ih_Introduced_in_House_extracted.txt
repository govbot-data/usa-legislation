        A BILL
        To repeal the Executive order entitled Ensuring a
                National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence.
    
    
        
            1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Guaranteeing and Upholding Americans’ Right to Decide Responsible AI Laws and Standards Act or as the GUARDRAILS Act.
        
        
            2.
            Repeal of executive order
 The Executive order entitled Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence, issued on December 11, 2025, shall have no force or effect, and no Federal funds may be used to implement, administer, enforce, or carry out that Executive order.
