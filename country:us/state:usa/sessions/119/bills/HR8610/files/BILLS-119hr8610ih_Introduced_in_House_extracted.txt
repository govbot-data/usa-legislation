R. 8610IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 30, 2026Mr. Fallon (for himself and Mr. Walkinshaw) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo amend the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007 to require agency reports on any procurement waiver made by the President. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Sudan Waiver Report Reduction Act. 2.Reporting requirement with respect to waivers granted under the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007Section 6(c)(2) of the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007 (Public Law 110–174; 50 U.S.C. 1701 note) is amended to read as follows: 
 
(2)Reporting requirementNot later than 30 days after the President grants a waiver under paragraph (1), the head of the executive agency concerned shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report on such waiver..
