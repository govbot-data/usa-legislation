RES. 134IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 12, 2025Ms. Ross (for herself, Mr. Veasey, and Mr. Krishnamoorthi) submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign AffairsJOINT RESOLUTIONTerminating the national emergency declared to impose duties on articles imported from India.That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (50 U.S.C. 1622), the national emergency declared on August 6, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14329 (90 Fed.
