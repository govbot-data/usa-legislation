RES. 99IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 10, 2025Ms. Rosen (for herself, Mr. Coons, Mr. King, Mr. Schiff, Mr. Durbin, Mr. Van Hollen, Mrs. Shaheen, Mr. Padilla, Mr. Welch, Ms. Cortez Masto, and Mr. Bennet) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services relating to Removal of the Automatic Extension of Employment Authorization Documents.That Congress disapproves the interim final rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services relating to Removal of the Automatic Extension of Employment Authorization Documents (90 Fed. Reg. 48799 (October 30, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
