II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4808IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJune 17, 2026Ms. Cortez Masto (for herself and Mr. Moreno) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsA BILLTo amend the Federal Credit Union Act with respect to conversion modernization for privately insured credit unions.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Privately Insured Credit Unions Conversion Modernization Act.2.Conversion modernization for privately 6 insured credit unionsSection 206(d)(2) of the Federal Credit Union Act (12 U.S.C. 1786(d)(2)) is amended—(1)in the first sentence, by striking in a vote in which at least 20 per centum of the total membership of the credit union participates; and(2)in the second sentence, by striking not more than thirty nor less than seven days and inserting not less than 90 days.
