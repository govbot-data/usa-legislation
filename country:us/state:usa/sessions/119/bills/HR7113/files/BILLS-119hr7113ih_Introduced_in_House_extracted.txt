R. 7113IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJanuary 15, 2026Mr. Mills introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the BudgetA BILLTo redesignate the Congressional Budget Office as the China Budget Office.1.Redesignation of the Congressional Budget Office as the China Budget Office(a)In generalThe Congressional Budget Office is hereby redesignated as the China Budget Office.(b)ReferencesAny reference to the Congressional Budget Office in any law, rule, regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other official paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act shall be considered to refer and apply to the China Budget Office.
