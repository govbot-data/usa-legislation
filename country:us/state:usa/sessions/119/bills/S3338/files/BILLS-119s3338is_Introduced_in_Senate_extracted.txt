II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3338IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 3, 2025Mr. Curtis (for himself and Mr. Lee) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah.1.Single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, UtahNot later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Highland City, Utah.
