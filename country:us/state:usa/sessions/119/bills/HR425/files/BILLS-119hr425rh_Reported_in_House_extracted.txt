enacting clause and insert the part printed in italicFor text of introduced bill, see copy of bill as introduced on January 15, 2025A BILLTo repeal the Corporate Transparency Act.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Repealing Big Brother Overreach Act.2.Beneficial ownership information reporting requirements(a)In generalSection 5336 of title 31, United States Code, is amended—(1)by inserting foreign after Beneficial each place such term appears in heading or text;(2)by inserting foreign after beneficial each place such term appears in heading or text (and conforming the item relating to such section 5336 in the table of contents for chapter 53 of title 31, United States Code, accordingly); and(3)in subsection (a)—(A)in paragraph (3)(A), by inserting after individual who the following: is a foreign person and; and(B)in paragraph (11)(A), by striking that is and all that follows through (ii) formed and inserting that is formed.(b)Deletion of data relating to non-reporting companiesNot later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network shall delete all beneficial ownership information collected pursuant to section 5336 of title 31, United States Code, with respect to—(1)any individual who is not a foreign beneficial owner (as such term is defined under such section 5336); or(2)a corporation, limited liability company, or other similar entity that is not a reporting company (as such term is defined under such section 5336).June 18, 2026Reported with an amendment, committed to the Committee of the Whole
