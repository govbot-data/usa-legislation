I119th CONGRESS2d SessionH. R. 7484IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 11, 2026Mr. Barr introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial ServicesA BILLTo amend the Electronic Fund Transfer Act to adjust certain thresholds for inflation. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Community Bank Relief Act. 2.Reasonable fees and rules for payment card transactionsSection 921(a)(6) of the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (15 U.S.C. 1693o–2(a)(6)) is amended—
(1)by redesignating subparagraph (B) as subparagraph (C); and (2)by inserting after subparagraph (A) the following:

(B)Inflation adjustments
(i)In generalExcept as provided in clause (ii), not later than July 1, 2026, and not later than January 15 of every year thereafter, the amount described in subparagraph (A) shall be adjusted by the annual percentage increase in the Consumer Price Index for the month of October. (ii)ExceptionPrior to the first adjustment made under clause (i), the Board shall adjust the amount described in subparagraph (A) by the percentage by which—

 the Consumer Price Index for October 2025, exceeds
                            
(II)the Consumer Price Index for October 2009..
