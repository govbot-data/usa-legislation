R. 8187IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 2, 2026Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and TechnologyA BILLTo amend title 51, United States Code, to direct the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration to seek to establish the initial elements of a lunar outpost, and for other purposes. 
1.Lunar outpostSection 70505 of title 51, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:  (c)ElementsNot later than December 31, 2030, the Administrator shall seek to establish the initial elements of the lunar outpost referred to in subsection (a)..
