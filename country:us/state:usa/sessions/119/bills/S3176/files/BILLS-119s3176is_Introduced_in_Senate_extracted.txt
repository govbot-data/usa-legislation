II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3176IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESNovember 18, 2025Mr. Lankford (for himself and Mr. Mullin) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo direct the United States Postal Service to designate single, unique ZIP Codes for certain Oklahoma communities, and for other purposes.1.Single, unique zip codes for certain Oklahoma communitiesNot later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for each of the following communities:(1)Hochatown, Oklahoma.(2)North Enid, Oklahoma.
