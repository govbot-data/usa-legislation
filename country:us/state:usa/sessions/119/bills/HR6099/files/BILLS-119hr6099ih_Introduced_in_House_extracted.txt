R. 6099IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 18, 2025Mr. Mannion (for himself, Mr. LaLota, Mr. Garbarino, Mr. Suozzi, Ms. Gillen, Ms. Meng, Ms. Velázquez, Mr. Goldman of New York, Mr. Nadler, Ms. Ocasio-Cortez, Mr. Torres of New York, Mr. Latimer, Mr. Riley of New York, Mr. Tonko, Mr. Langworthy, Ms. Tenney, Mr. Morelle, and Mr. Kennedy of New York) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2200 South Salina Street in Syracuse, New York, as the Wallie Howard Jr. Post Office Building. 

      1.
      Wallie Howard Jr. Post Office Building
      
        (a)
        Designation
 The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2200 South Salina Street in Syracuse, New York, shall be known and designated as the Wallie Howard Jr. Post Office Building.
      
      
        (b)
        References
 Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Wallie Howard Jr. Post Office Building.
