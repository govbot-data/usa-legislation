II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3321IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 3, 2025Ms. Ernst (for herself and Ms. Hassan) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and PensionsA BILLTo amend the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993, to repeal certain limits on leave for married individuals employed by the same employer.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the FAIR Leave Act or the Fair Access for Individuals to Receive Leave Act.2.Repeal of certain time limitations on leave for spousesSection 102(f) of the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993 (29 U.S.C. 2612(f)) is repealed.
