R. 8756IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 12, 2026Mr. Moore of Alabama (for himself, Mr. Ogles, Mr. Brecheen, and Mr. Fuller) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo require certain aliens to disavow sharia law and take an oath to uphold the Constitution, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Countering Radicalism Under Sharia And Defeating Extremism Act or the CRUSADE Act.2.Special immigrant visa oathSection 203(b)(4) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1153(b)(4)) is amended by inserting after the period at the end the following: To be eligible for a visa issued in accordance with this paragraph, an alien shall be required to disavow sharia law and take an oath to uphold the Constitution..
