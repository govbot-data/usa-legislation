I119th CONGRESS2d SessionH. R. 8974IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 21, 2026Mr. Issa (for himself and Ms. Salazar) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign AffairsA BILLTo authorize the Development Finance Corporation to invest in Venezuela. 
1.Authorization to invest in Venezuela 
(a)Removal as country of concernSection 1402(3) of the BUILD Act of 2018 (22 U.S.C. 9601(3)) is amended— (1)by striking subparagraph (A); and 
(2)by redesignating subparagraphs (B) through (G) as subparagraphs (A) through (F), respectively. (b)Investment authorityNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Development Finance Corporation may invest in the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela.
