RES. 63IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 28, 2025Mr. Cline (for himself and Mr. Jackson of Illinois) submitted the following concurrent resolution; which was referred to the Committee on House AdministrationCONCURRENT RESOLUTIONAuthorizing the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for the Congressional National Prayer Breakfast. 
1.Use of rotunda for Congressional National Prayer Breakfast 
(a)Authorization 
(1)In generalThe rotunda of the United States Capitol is authorized to be used on February 5, 2026, for the Congressional National Prayer Breakfast. (2)Food and beverage serviceThe authorization provided under paragraph (1) shall include the service of food and beverages at the event described in such paragraph. 
(b)PreparationsPhysical preparations for the conduct of the event described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the Architect of the Capitol.
