R. 9025IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 26, 2026Mr. Finstad (for himself, Mr. Emmer, Mr. Stauber, Mrs. Fischbach, Ms. Morrison, Ms. Craig, and Ms. McCollum) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 1st Avenue Southeast in Austin, Minnesota, as the John Madden Memorial Post Office. 
1.John Madden Memorial Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 1st Avenue Southeast in Austin, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the John Madden Memorial Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the John Madden Memorial Post Office.
