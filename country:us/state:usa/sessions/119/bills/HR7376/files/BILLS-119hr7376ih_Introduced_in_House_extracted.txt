R. 7376IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 4, 2026Ms. Scholten (for herself and Mr. Mast) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and InfrastructureA BILLTo amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to reauthorize certain programs relating to nonpoint source management, and for other purposes. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Local Water Protection Act. 2.Nonpoint source management programsSection 319(j) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1329(j)) is amended by striking for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2027 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031.
