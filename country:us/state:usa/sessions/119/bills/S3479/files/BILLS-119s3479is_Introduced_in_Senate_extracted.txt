II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3479IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 15, 2025Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself and Mrs. Blackburn) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on FinanceA BILLTo specify that the Council on American-Islamic Relations shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Tax Exemptions For Terror Act.2.Council on American-Islamic relations subject to taxation(a)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Council on American-Islamic Relations shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.(b)Effective dateThis section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
