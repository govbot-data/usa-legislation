R. 5890IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESOctober 31, 2025Mr. Roy (for himself, Mr. Fine, Mr. Self, Mr. Ogles, Mr. Higgins of Louisiana, and Mr. Donalds) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and MeansA BILLTo specify that the Council on American-Islamic Relations and similar organizations shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Tax Exemptions For Terror Act. 2.Council on American-Islamic relations and similar organizations subject to taxation  (a) In general Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Council on American-Islamic Relations, or any other organization found to have ties to terrorism or terrorist organizations, shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.
       
(b)Effective dateThis section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
