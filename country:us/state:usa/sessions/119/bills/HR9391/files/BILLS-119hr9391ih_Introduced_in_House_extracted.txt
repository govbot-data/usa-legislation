R. 9391IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 23, 2026Mr. Garbarino (for himself and Ms. Pou) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Homeland SecurityA BILLTo authorize an extension and expansion of the reimbursable screening services program of the Transportation Security Administration, and for other purposes. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Reimbursable Screening Services Program Extension Act of 2026. 
2.Extension and expansion of reimbursable screening services program 
(a)ExtensionSubsection (e) of section 225 of division A of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2019 (Public Law 116–6; 49 U.S.C. 44901 note), is amended by striking through fiscal year 2026 and inserting through fiscal year 2031. (b)ExpansionSubsection (e) of section 225 of division A of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2019 is further amended by striking not more than eight and inserting not more than 14.
