A BILL
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to allow for Federal financial participation for services furnished to patients in an institution for mental diseases under the Medicaid program.

 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Restoring Inpatient Mental Health Access Act of 2025.   2.Allowing for Federal financial participation for services furnished to patients in an institution for mental diseases under the Medicaid program (a)In generalSection 1905(a) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396d(a)) is amended— 
(1)in paragraph (1), by striking (other than services in an institution for mental diseases); (2)in paragraph (4)(A), by striking (other than services in an institution for mental diseases); 
(3)by striking paragraph (14); (4)in paragraph (15), by striking (other than services in an institution for mental diseases); and 
(5)in the matter designated as subparagraph (B) following the last numbered paragraph of such subsection, by inserting furnished before January 1, 2027, after care or services. (b)Conforming amendmentSection 1915(l)(1) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396n(l)(1)) is amended by inserting and before January 1, 2027, after October 1, 2019,. 
(c)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to medical assistance furnished on or after January 1, 2027.
