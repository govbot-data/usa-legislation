R. 7663IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESFebruary 24, 2026Mr. Ogles introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo amend section 6103 of title 5, United States Code, to change the date designated for Washington’s Birthday and designate Lincoln’s Birthday as a legal public holiday. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Restoring Washington's Birthday Act of 2026. 
2.Changes to legal public holidaysSection 6103(a) of title 5, United States Code, is amended— 
(1)by inserting after the item relating to the Birthday of Martin Luther King, Jr., the following:  Lincoln’s Birthday.; and 
(2)in the item relating to Washington’s Birthday, by striking the third Monday in February and inserting February 22.
