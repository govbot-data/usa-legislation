R. 9269IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 11, 2026Mr. Clyburn (for himself, Mr. Carey, Ms. Sewell, Mr. Fleischmann, Mr. Davis of Illinois, Mr. Fitzpatrick, Mr. Bell, Mr. Cohen, and Mr. McCormick) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural ResourcesA BILLTo extend the authorization of the African American Civil Rights Network. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Renewing the African American Civil Rights Network Act. 2.Extension of authorizationSection 308404 of title 54, United States Code, is amended by striking 7 years after the date of enactment of this chapter and inserting 10 years after the date of the enactment of the Renewing the African American Civil Rights Network Act.
