R. 6749IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 16, 2025Mrs. Houchin introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo codify Executive Order 14243 (relating to stopping waste, fraud, and abuse by eliminating information silos). 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Eliminating Information Silos Act of 2025. 2.Codification of Executive Order 14243Executive Order 14243 (90 Fed. Reg. 13681; relating to stopping waste, fraud, and abuse by eliminating information silos) shall have the force and effect of law.
