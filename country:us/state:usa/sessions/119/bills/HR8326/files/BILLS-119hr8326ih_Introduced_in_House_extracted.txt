R. 8326IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 16, 2026Mr. Conaway (for himself, Mrs. McIver, Mr. Pallone, Mr. Menendez, Mrs. Watson Coleman, and Mr. Kean) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16 Rev Dr Martin Luther King Jr Drive in Willingboro, New Jersey, as the James A. Cotten Post Office. 
1.James A. Cotten Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16 Rev Dr Martin Luther King Jr Drive in Willingboro, New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the James A. Cotten Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the James A. Cotten Post Office.
