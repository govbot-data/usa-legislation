II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4146IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 19, 2026Ms. Lummis (for herself, Mr. Barrasso, Mr. Sheehy, Mr. Ricketts, and Mr. Daines) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public WorksA BILLTo amend the Endangered Species Act of 1973 with respect to the listing of threatened and endangered species, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Local Data for Better Conservation Act.2.Listing determinationsSection 4 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1533) is amended by adding at the end the following:(j)State dataIn making a determination to list, or delist, a species as a threatened species or an endangered species under this section, the Secretary shall accept and integrate State-collected data..
