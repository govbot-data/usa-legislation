1.Specialist Jeffrey L. White, Jr. Post Office Redesignation 
(a)RedesignationSection 1(a) of Public Law 115–134 (132 Stat. 340) is amended by striking 324 and inserting 211. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 of Public Law 115–134, as amended by subsection (a), shall be deemed to be a reference to the Specialist Jeffrey L. White, Jr. Post Office.
