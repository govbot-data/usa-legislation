 ;(2)by striking sections 1423 to 1428 and inserting sections 1423, 1424, and 1426 to 1428; and(3)by adding at the end the following:(b)No limitationNotwithstanding any other law, an indictment may be found or an information instituted at any time without limitation for any offense under section 1425..
