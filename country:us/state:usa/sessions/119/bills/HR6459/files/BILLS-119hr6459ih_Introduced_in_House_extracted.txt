R. 6459IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 4, 2025Ms. Maloy introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 35 South Main Street in Glendale, Utah, as the Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office. 
1.Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 35 South Main Street in Glendale, Utah, shall be known and designated as the Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office.
