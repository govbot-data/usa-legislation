R. 9741IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJuly 16, 2026Mr. Garcia of California introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo name the District of Columbia tuition assistance grant program the Eleanor Holmes Norton District of Columbia Tuition Assistance Grant Program. 
1.Name of District of Columbia tuition assistance grant programThe District of Columbia tuition assistance grant program established by the District of Columbia College Access Act of 1999 (sec. 38–2701 et seq., D.C. Official Code) shall on and after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Eleanor Holmes Norton District of Columbia Tuition Assistance Grant Program. Any reference to the District of Columbia tuition assistance grant program in any law, regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Eleanor Holmes Norton District of Columbia Tuition Assistance Grant Program.
