1.Reauthorization of school-based health centers grant programSection 399Z–1(m) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 280h–5(m)) is amended by striking such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2022 through 2026 and inserting $55,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031.
