R. 7864IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMarch 9, 2026Ms. Budzinski (for herself, Mr. Bost, Mr. Bell, and Mrs. Wagner) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural ResourcesA BILLTo amend the Act of May 17, 1954, to revise the boundary of Gateway Arch National Park. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Gateway Arch National Park Boundary Revision Act of 2026. 2.Boundary revisionSection 4(a) of the Act of May 17, 1954 (16 U.S.C. 450jj–3(a); 68 Stat. 99) is amended— 
(1)by striking one hundred acres and inserting 102.18 acres; and  (2)by striking map entitled and all that follows through January 1992 and inserting map titled Gateway Arch National Park Proposed Boundary Revision, numbered 366/189,627, dated August 2023.
