119th CONGRESS2d Session
H. R. 6247
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
AN ACT
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office.


1.Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office.
Passed the House of Representatives April 14, 2026.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
