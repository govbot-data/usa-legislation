R. 6247IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 21, 2025Mr. Ciscomani (for himself, Mr. Stanton, Ms. Ansari, Mr. Biggs of Arizona, Mr. Crane, Mr. Gosar, Mrs. Grijalva, Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona, and Mr. Schweikert) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office.1.Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office.
