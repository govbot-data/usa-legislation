R. 6059IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 17, 2025Mr. Kennedy of Utah introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 50 East 100 North in Moab, Utah, as the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office. 
1.2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 50 East 100 North in Moab, Utah, as the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office.
