II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4072IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 12, 2026Mr. Schmitt introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and ForestryA BILLTo reauthorize Agricultural and Food Policy Research Centers.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Agricultural and Food Policy Research Centers Reauthorization Act of 2026.2.Reauthorization of Agricultural and Food Policy Research CentersSection 1419A(e) of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 (7 U.S.C. 3155(e)) is amended by striking $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2023 and inserting $15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031.
