R. 9254IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 10, 2026Mr. Roy (for himself, Mr. Hunt, Mr. Brecheen, Ms. Boebert, and Mr. Harris of North Carolina) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and MeansA BILLTo specify that the Southern Poverty Law Center shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Stop Subsidizing Political Lawfare by Charities Act of 2026 or the Stop the SPLC Act of 2026. 
2.Southern Poverty Law Center subject to taxation 
(a)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Southern Poverty Law Center shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. (b)Effective dateThis section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
