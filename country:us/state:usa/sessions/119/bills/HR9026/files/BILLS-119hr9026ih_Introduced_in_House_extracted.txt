R. 9026IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESMay 26, 2026Ms. Gillen (for herself, Mr. Garbarino, Mr. Suozzi, Mr. LaLota, Mr. Torres of New York, Mr. Lawler, Mr. Mannion, Ms. Tenney, Ms. Meng, Ms. Malliotakis, Mr. Kennedy of New York, Mr. Jeffries, Mr. Latimer, Mr. Riley of New York, Mr. Morelle, Mr. Goldman of New York, Ms. Stefanik, Mr. Langworthy, Ms. Clarke of New York, and Ms. Ocasio-Cortez) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 80 Atlantic Avenue in Oceanside, New York, as the Detective Luis G. 1.Detective Luis G. Alvarez Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 80 Atlantic Avenue in Oceanside, New York, shall be known and designated as the Detective Luis G. Alvarez Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Detective Luis G. Alvarez Post Office.
