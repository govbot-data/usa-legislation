CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 
Authorizing the use of Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center for an event to unveil the statue of Barbara Rose Johns. 
 
 
1.Use of Emancipation Hall for unveiling of Barbara Rose Johns statue 
(a)AuthorizationEmancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used for an event on December 16, 2025, for an event to unveil the statue of Barbara Rose Johns. (b)PreparationsPhysical preparations for the conduct of the event described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the Architect of the Capitol. 
 
Passed the House of Representatives November 20, 2025.Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
