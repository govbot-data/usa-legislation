I119th CONGRESS2d SessionH. R. 8634IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESApril 30, 2026Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the JudiciaryA BILLTo prohibit marriages between individuals who are closely related by blood. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Consanguineous Marriage Prohibition Act of 2026. 2.Federal prohibition on consanguineous marriage Section 7 of title 1, United States Code, is amended by striking 2 individuals and inserting 2 individuals who are not related by affinity or consanguinity within the degree of first cousin each place it appears.
