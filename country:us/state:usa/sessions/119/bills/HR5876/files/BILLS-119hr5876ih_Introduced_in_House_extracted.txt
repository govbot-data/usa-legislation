R. 5876IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESOctober 31, 2025Mr. Figures (for himself, Mr. Johnson of Georgia, Ms. Pou, and Mr. Carson) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo provide for continuity in certain contracts during a lapse in appropriations, and for other purposes. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Keep America Building Act. 2.Continuity of contractsNo Federal funds may be used to suspend, delay, or interrupt all or part of the work of a project under a contract during any lapse in appropriations or stop all or any part of the work agreed to in such contract.
