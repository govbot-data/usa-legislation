II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 3327IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 3, 2025Mr. Lee introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo repeal the Human Rights Sanctuary Amendment Act of 2022.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the D.C. Shield Law Repeal Act.2.Repeal of Human Rights Sanctuary Amendment Act of 2022The Human Rights Sanctuary Amendment Act of 2022 (D.C. Law 24–257) is hereby repealed, and any provision of law amended or repealed by such Act is restored or revived as if such Act had not been enacted into law.
