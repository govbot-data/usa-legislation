II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4182IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 24, 2026Mr. Welch (for himself, Mr. Justice, Mr. Schiff, and Mrs. Hyde-Smith) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and ForestryA BILLTo amend the Farm Credit Act of 1971 to modify rural housing financing under that Act.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Fostering the Availability in Rural Markets of Home Loans Act of 2026 or the FARM Home Loans Act of 2026.2.Rural housing financingSection 1.11(b) of the Farm Credit Act of 1971 (12 U.S.C. 2019(b)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (2), by striking appurtenances and inserting appurtenances, including accessory dwelling units,; and(2)in paragraph (3), by striking 2,500 and inserting 10,000.
