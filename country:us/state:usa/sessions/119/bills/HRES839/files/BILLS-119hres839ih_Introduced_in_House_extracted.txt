IV119th CONGRESS1st SessionH. RES. 839IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESOctober 28, 2025Mr. Gottheimer (for himself and Mr. Crawford) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign AffairsRESOLUTIONCondemning Hamas for assassinating innocent Palestinians. 
Whereas, since the implementation of the ceasefire agreement on October 10, 2025, Hamas has carried out a campaign of executions and intimidation against innocent Palestinians in Gaza as it seeks to suppress dissent and reassert control over the territory, resulting in the deaths of scores of civilians; and Whereas Hamas is designated as a Foreign Terrorist Organization by the United States: Now, therefore, be it  
That the House of Representatives— (1)condemns in the strongest terms the killings and acts of terror committed by Hamas against innocent Palestinians in Gaza; and 
(2)reaffirms the commitment of the United States to supporting the implementation of the ceasefire agreement and to advancing stability and peace for innocent civilians in Gaza.
