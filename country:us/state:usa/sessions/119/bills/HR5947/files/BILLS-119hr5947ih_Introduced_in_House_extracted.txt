R. 5947IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESNovember 7, 2025Mr. Flood introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1201 Calvert Street in Lincoln, Nebraska, as the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building. 
1.Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1201 Calvert Street in Lincoln, Nebraska, shall be known and designated as the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building.
