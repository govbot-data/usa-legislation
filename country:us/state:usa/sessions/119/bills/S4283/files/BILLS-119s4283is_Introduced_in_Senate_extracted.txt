II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4283IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESApril 14, 2026Ms. Baldwin (for herself and Ms. Collins) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and PensionsA BILLTo amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize a public health and bio-preparedness workforce loan repayment program.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment Reauthorization Act of 2026.2.Reauthorization of Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment ProgramSection 776(i) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 295f–1(i)) is amended by striking fiscal years 2023 through 2025 and inserting fiscal years 2027 through 2031.
