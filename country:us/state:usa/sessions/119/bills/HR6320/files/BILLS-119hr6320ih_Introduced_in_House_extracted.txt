(1)in section 1902(a)(10)(A), in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking and (30) and inserting (30), and (32); (2)in section 1905— 
(A)in subsection (a)— (i)in paragraph (31), by striking ; and and inserting a semicolon; 
(ii)by redesignating paragraph (32) as paragraph (33); and (iii)by inserting the following paragraph after paragraph (31): 
 
(32)lung cancer biomarker testing; and; and (3)in section 1937(b)(5), by inserting before the period at the end the following: , and beginning January 1, 2027, coverage of lung cancer biomarker testing.  
(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall apply beginning January 1, 2027.
