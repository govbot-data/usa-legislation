II119th CONGRESS2d SessionS. 4745IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJune 10, 2026Mr. Husted (for himself and Mr. Moreno) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Grant Avenue in Georgetown, Ohio, as the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building.1.Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Grant Avenue in Georgetown, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building.
