R. 9521IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 29, 2026Mr. McDowell (for himself, Mr. Edwards, and Mr. Moore of North Carolina) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo amend title VI of the Social Security Act to extend that date on which certain funds related to broadband infrastructure projects may be expended. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Broadband Infrastructure Extension Act. 
2.Extension of deadlines for certain fiscal recovery and critical capital projects funds 
(a)State fiscal recovery fundSection 602(c)(5)(E) of the Social Security Act is amended by adding at the end the following: Notwithstanding the previous sentence, such funds provided for projects related to broadband infrastructure may be expended through September 30, 2027.. (b)Local fiscal recovery fundSection 603(c)(6)(D) of the Social Security Act is amended by adding at the end the following: Notwithstanding the previous sentence, such funds provided for projects related to broadband infrastructure may be expended through September 30, 2027.. 
(c)Capital projects fundSection 604 of the Social Security Act is amended by— (1)redesignating subsection (d) as subsection (e); and 
(2)by inserting after subsection (c) the following:  (d)AvailabilityFunds provided under a payment made under this section for a project related to broadband infrastructure may be expended prior to January 1, 2028..
