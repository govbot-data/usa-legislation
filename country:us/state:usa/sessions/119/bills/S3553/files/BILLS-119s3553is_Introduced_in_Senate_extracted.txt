II119th CONGRESS
 1st SessionS. 3553IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESDecember 17, 2025Ms. Ernst introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental AffairsA BILLTo require the Director of the Office of Personnel Management to establish a searchable, publicly available website that contains information regarding the Federal civilian workforce, and for other purposes.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Where’s the Workforce At Listed by Duties and Office or the Where’s WALDO Act.2.OPM responsibilities(a)DefinitionsIn this section:(1)Civil serviceThe term civil service has the meaning given the term in section 2101 of title 5, United States Code.(2)Contract employeeThe term contract employee means—(A)an employee of a contractor; or(B)in the case of a contractor who is an individual who directly enters into a contract with the Federal Government, that individual.(3)ContractorThe term contractor has the meaning given the term in section 7101 of title 41, United States Code.(4)DirectorThe term Director means the Director of the Office of Personnel Management.(b)WebsiteNot later than 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director shall establish, and thereafter the Director shall maintain, a searchable, publicly available website that contains information regarding each individual occupying a position in the civil service, which shall include, with respect to each such individual—(1)the title of the position occupied by the individual;(2)a description of the duties of the position occupied by the individual;(3)the employing agency of the individual;(4)the primary duty station of the individual;(5)the annual rate of basic pay of the individual, including, with respect to a year, any bonus or supplemental wages paid to the individual; and(6)the date on which the individual was appointed to the position occupied by the individual.(c)Annual report(1)In generalNot later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter, the Director shall make publicly available a report that details, for the year covered by the report—(A)the total number of contract employees that worked, on a part-time or full-time basis, under applicable contracts with the Federal Government; and(B)the total cost incurred by the Federal Government with respect to the work described in subparagraph (A).(2)Breakdown of dataThe data contained in each report made available under paragraph (1) shall be disaggregated by agency.
