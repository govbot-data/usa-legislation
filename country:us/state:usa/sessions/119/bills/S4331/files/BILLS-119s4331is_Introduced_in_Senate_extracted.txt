investment became part of the investment hedging unit.(D)Built-in gainFor purposes of this section, the term built-in gain means, with respect to any underlying investment in an investment hedging that the investment hedging unit was established or the time that the underlying investment became part of the investment hedging unit.(4)Special transition rules for REITS electing to include certain
