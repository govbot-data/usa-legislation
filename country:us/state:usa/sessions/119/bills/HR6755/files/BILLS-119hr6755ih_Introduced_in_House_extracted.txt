R. 6755IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESDecember 16, 2025Ms. Mace introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' AffairsA BILLTo amend title 38, United States Code, to relax restrictions on filling certain vacant positions at the Department of Veterans Affairs. 
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Accountable Leadership for Veterans Act of 2025.  2.Senior Executive Service positions in the Department filled by noncareer appointeesSection 709(a)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking 5 percent and inserting 10 percent. 
3.Under Secretary for HealthSection 305 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:  305.Under Secretary for HealthThere is in the Department an Under Secretary for Health, who is appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.. 
4.Under Secretary for BenefitsSection 306 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:  306.Under Secretary for BenefitsThere is in the Department an Under Secretary for Benefits, who is appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate..
